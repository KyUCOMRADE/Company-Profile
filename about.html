About Danchega Builders Ltd

Danchega Builders Ltd is a registered private company based in Murang’a, Kenya. We are licensed under the Company Act 2015 and hold NCA serial number NCA/SS/2018/21579.

Our director, Daniel Chege, brings extensive experience in construction, design, and project management — with exposure to international building standards and modern practices.

We are passionate about transforming ideas into structures that stand the test of time. Our mission is to build with integrity, precision, and professionalism — ensuring client satisfaction in every project.

Our Core Values

  • 🏗️ Quality: Delivering durable workmanship and elegant finishes.
  • 🤝 Integrity: Transparent communication and ethical project management.
  • 🦺 Safety: Ensuring compliance with safety and regulatory standards.
  • 👷 Client Focus: Personalized solutions that exceed expectations.
  • 💡 Innovation: Integrating modern construction technologies and designs.

Meet the Visionary

Daniel Chege, the founder and director of Danchega Builders Ltd, leads the company with a vision to redefine construction standards in Kenya. His leadership combines technical expertise, creative problem-solving, and a deep commitment to client satisfaction.

Under his guidance, the company has successfully delivered multiple projects — from home construction to commercial renovations — each reflecting Danchega Builders’ signature of excellence.
